Our Story

Who We Are

A Family Business
Built on Honest Work

Butler Build & Design is run by Daniel Butler — a Marine Corps veteran and soon-to-be graduate student at the University of Tennessee — and his wife Lindsay. What started with a passion for house-flipping grew into a full-fledged remodeling business grounded in one simple belief: people deserve to have someone they can genuinely trust working in their home.

Daniel's background as an officer and his hands-on experience flipping homes give him a rare combination of disciplined execution and an eye for what makes a space truly sing. Lindsay brings warmth, design sensibility, and a homeowner's perspective to every project.

Our Values

Everything We Do

01

Honesty & Transparency

We hear it all the time: "Who can I trust?" That's why every invoice is clear, every rate is flat, and every material is billed at exactly what we pay — no markups, ever.

Respect for Your Home

Your home is still your home during a project. We work clean, minimize disruption, and treat every space as if it were our own. You won't find dust sneaking under your doors.

Full Home Remodels

From kitchens and bathrooms to living rooms and bedrooms, we handle complete transformations with a meticulous eye for finish quality. Whether you're upgrading to sell or investing in the home you plan to grow old in, we deliver spaces that feel intentional and refined.

  • Kitchen & bath remodels
  • Flooring installation & refinishing
  • Tile, trim, & finish carpentry
  • Painting & interior finishes

Renovation & Repair

Not everything needs a full remodel — sometimes the right fix in the right place makes all the difference. We take on repairs and renovations of all scopes, always with the same level of care and attention to detail.

  • Structural repairs & updates
  • Drywall, texture & patching
  • Door & window installation
  • Deck & exterior work
